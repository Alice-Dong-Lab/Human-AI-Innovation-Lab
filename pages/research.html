Our Research

Pioneering innovations in human-AI collaboration and responsible AI development

Human-AI Collaboration Systems

We develop interactive systems that enable humans and AI to work synergistically. Our research focuses on designing interfaces and workflows that enhance human decision-making while leveraging AI's computational advantages.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Interactive machine learning interfaces
  • Human-in-the-loop AI systems
  • Adaptive AI for personalized collaboration
  • User experience design for AI systems

AI Safety and Robustness

Our team investigates methods to ensure AI systems are robust, safe, and reliable in real-world applications. We explore adversarial robustness, verification methods, and safety guarantees.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Adversarial robustness testing
  • Formal verification of AI systems
  • Safety constraints and alignment
  • Uncertainty quantification in AI

Interpretable and Explainable AI

We create methods to make AI decisions transparent and understandable to humans. This is crucial for building trust and enabling effective human oversight in critical domains.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Feature importance and saliency methods
  • Model-agnostic explanation techniques
  • Concept-based interpretability
  • Fairness and bias detection

AI Ethics and Societal Impact

We examine the broader implications of AI on society and develop frameworks for ethical AI deployment. Our research informs policy and best practices for responsible AI development.

Key Focus Areas:

  • AI fairness and bias mitigation
  • Privacy-preserving machine learning
  • Societal impact assessment
  • Governance and policy recommendations
