About Our Lab

The Human-AI Innovation Lab focuses on cutting-edge research at the intersection of human cognition and artificial intelligence. We are dedicated to developing AI systems that enhance human capabilities, promote ethical AI practices, and foster meaningful human-AI collaboration.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between human intelligence and machine learning, creating innovative solutions that benefit society while maintaining a strong commitment to responsible AI development.

Research Areas

Human-AI Collaboration

Designing systems where humans and AI work together effectively, enhancing decision-making and productivity.

AI Safety & Ethics

Developing frameworks and methodologies to ensure AI systems are safe, fair, transparent, and aligned with human values.

Interpretable AI

Creating explainable AI models that humans can understand and trust in high-stakes applications.

Social Impact

Researching the societal implications of AI and developing solutions for equitable AI deployment.
