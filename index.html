About the Lab

The Human-AI Innovation Lab, directed by Dr. Alice Dong, focuses on cutting-edge research at the intersection of human cognition and artificial intelligence. Our mission is to develop AI systems that enhance human capabilities while maintaining strong commitments to ethical practices and responsible innovation.

We are dedicated to advancing the field through collaborative research, mentoring the next generation of AI researchers, and creating practical solutions that address real-world challenges in human-AI collaboration.

Lab Mission

To pioneer human-centered AI systems that are safe, interpretable, and beneficial to society through rigorous research and innovative collaboration.

Research Focus Areas

🤝

Human-AI Collaboration

Designing interactive systems where humans and AI enhance each other's capabilities, creating synergistic partnerships for improved decision-making and problem-solving.

  • Interactive machine learning interfaces
  • Human-in-the-loop AI systems
  • Adaptive personalization
🛡️

AI Safety & Ethics

Developing frameworks and methodologies to ensure AI systems are safe, fair, transparent, and aligned with human values and societal needs.

  • Robustness and adversarial testing
  • Fairness and bias mitigation
  • AI alignment and control
🔍

Interpretable AI

Creating explainable AI models that humans can understand and trust, enabling effective oversight in critical applications.

  • Explanation methods and saliency
  • Model interpretability
  • Trust and transparency
🌍

Societal Impact

Examining AI's broader implications on society and developing frameworks for ethical deployment that benefits all communities.

  • Impact assessment
  • Policy recommendations
  • Equitable AI deployment
